#### 7.5 Editorial Style Guide

**Prohibited Terms:**
- "100% secure", "unhackable", "guaranteed protection"
- "Revolutionary" (use "innovative" or "advanced")
- "Game-changing" (use "significant" or "transformative")
- Marketing hyperbole without evidence

**Preferred Alternatives:**
- "Significantly reduces attack surface" vs "eliminates risk"
- "Demonstrated capability through XYZ" vs "guaranteed results"
- "87% prediction accuracy validated over 36 months" vs "perfect predictions"

**Voice & Tone:**
- **Professional yet visionary**: Grounded in evidence, inspired by potential
- **Evidence-based**: Every claim supported by data or research
- **Customer-centric**: Focus on solving real problems
- **Technically sophisticated**: Respect audience expertise
- **Confident but humble**: Acknowledge limitations and areas for improvement
