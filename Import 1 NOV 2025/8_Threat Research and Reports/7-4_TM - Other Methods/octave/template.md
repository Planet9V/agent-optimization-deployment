## Template Usage Guide

### Preparation Phase
1. **Assemble Team:** Identify workshop participants and facilitators
2. **Gather Materials:** Collect organizational documents and data
3. **Schedule Workshops:** Book workshop dates and facilities
4. **Prepare Materials:** Customize template for organization

### Workshop Execution
1. **Workshop 1:** Focus on strategic alignment and asset identification
2. **Inter-workshop:** Complete Phase 2 vulnerability analysis
3. **Workshop 2:** Review technology infrastructure and vulnerabilities
4. **Inter-workshop:** Develop Phase 3 security strategy
5. **Workshop 3:** Finalize risk assessment and implementation plans

### Post-Workshop Activities
1. **Document Final Report:** Complete all template sections
2. **Gain Approvals:** Obtain stakeholder sign-off
3. **Create Action Plans:** Develop detailed implementation plans
4. **Establish Monitoring:** Set up metrics and review processes
