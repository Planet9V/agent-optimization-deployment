## I. Regulatory Framework Analysis

### A. Competitive Bidding Requirements

#### 1. 47 CFR ยง 54.503 - Competitive Bidding Requirements
**Relevant Case: SERRC/GCI Communications Monopoly**
- Market Share: 93.83% in Alaska
- Legal Analysis:
  * Violation of ยง 54.503(a): "All entities participating in the schools and libraries program must conduct a fair and open competitive bidding process."
  * Evidence:
    - Single provider dominance
    - Limited competition
    - Sustained market control
    - Geographic isolation

*Precedent:* In re Request for Review by Mastermind Internet Services, Inc., 16 FCC Rcd 4028 (2000)
- Established standard for competitive bidding
- Required documentation of multiple bids
- Emphasized price as primary factor
