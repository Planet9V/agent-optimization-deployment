
### **Archaea Energy Business Profile**  
**Parent Company:** BP plc (acquired December 2022)  
**Headquarters:** Houston, Texas  
**Sector:** Renewable Natural Gas (RNG) Production  
**Key Facilities:**  
- 50+ RNG plants across 32 U.S. states, including Pennsylvania, Kansas, and Indiana (BP, 2024a; BP, 2024b).  
- Modular Archaea Modular Design (AMD) plants:  
  - **Medora, IN**: 3,200 scfm capacity (BP, 2023).  
  - **Shawnee, KS**: 9,600 scfm capacity, largest to date (BP, 2024a).  
**Revenue Model:**  
- Long-term RNG contracts with utilities and industrial partners (e.g., Northwest Natural Gas Company) (Archaea Energy, 2021).  
- Federal/state renewable fuel credits (Renewable Fuel Standard, Low Carbon Fuel Standard) (BP, 2024c).  
**Mission:** *"Maximize RNG to decarbonize high-emission industries"* (BP, 2024c).  

---

### **Services**  
1. **RNG Production**  
   - Converts landfill gas (methane/CO₂) into pipeline-quality RNG using membrane separation technology (BP, 2024c).  
   - Applications: Replaces diesel in transportation, industrial heating, and power generation (BP, 2024c).  

2. **Modular Plant Development**  
   - AMD plants built on skids with interchangeable components, reducing deployment time by 30% vs. traditional builds (BP, 2023; BP, 2024a).  

3. **Carbon Capture & Storage (CCS)**  
   - Pilot projects to sequester CO₂ separated during RNG production (BP, 2024c).  

4. **Partnerships**  
   - **Lightning Renewables JV**: 39 RNG facilities with Republic Services, targeting $1.1B investment (BP, 2024a; Waste Dive, 2024).  
   - **UGI Corporation**: Co-developing RNG facilities in Pennsylvania (UGI, 2023).

### **Recent News**  
1. **Shawnee, KS Plant Launch (April 2024)**  
   - Largest AMD plant (9,600 scfm), heats ~38,000 homes annually (BP, 2024a; EnergyTech, 2024).  

2. **Fort Wayne, IN Plant Inauguration (June 2024)**  
   - Processes 6,400 scfm landfill gas via Lightning Renewables JV (Hart Energy, 2024; Waste Dive, 2024).  

3. **Pennsylvania Expansion (August 2024)**  
   - New RNG plant at Bethlehem Landfill (3,500 scfm capacity) with Waste Connections (American Biogas Council, 2024).  

4. **Financial Targets**  
   - BP aims for $4B EBITDA from bioenergy by 2030, with Archaea contributing 50M MMBtu/year RNG (BP, 2023; Renewable Energy Magazine, 2023).
