## Common Elements Across All Briefs

### Threat Intelligence Features
- **Predictive Detection:** 90-180 days advance warning on APT campaigns and ransomware
- **Attribution Analysis:** Nation-state actor psychometric profiling and TTP mapping
- **Geopolitical Context:** Regional threat landscape and strategic intelligence
- **Dark Web Monitoring:** Credential exposure, data leaks, infrastructure mentions

### AEON Solution Components
- **Digital Twin:** Interactive visualization of infrastructure, attack surfaces, vulnerabilities
- **Agent Zero Red Team:** Realistic adversary simulation using known APT TTPs
- **Continuous Intelligence:** Real-time threat feeds (STIX/TAXII) integrated with SIEM/SOC
- **Compliance Support:** Industry-specific regulatory frameworks (NERC CIP, HIPAA, PCI DSS, CMMC, etc.)

### Service Tier Structure (Consistent Across Industries)
- **Tier 1 (Foundation):** Digital twin development, vulnerability assessment, baseline ($280K-$850K)
- **Tier 2 (Operational Intelligence):** Continuous threat monitoring, monthly briefings ($120K-$250K/year)
- **Tier 3 (Advanced Defense):** Quarterly red team exercises, purple team collaboration ($380K-$600K/year)
- **Tier 4 (Specialized):** Industry-specific programs (IP protection, fraud, insider threat, etc.) ($100K-$220K/year)
- **Tier 5 (Strategic):** Executive advisory, regulatory compliance, strategic intelligence ($150K-$300K/year)

### Success Story Pattern
Each brief includes a detailed success story with:
- **Organization Profile:** Size, scope, complexity
- **Challenge:** Specific threats and business drivers
- **Implementation:** 4-phase approach over 18-20 months
- **Results:** Quantified business outcomes (incidents prevented, ROI, new revenue)
- **Testimonials:** CISO and CEO perspectives on business value

### ROI Calculation Methodology
- **Cost of Breach Scenarios:** 4 realistic scenarios per industry with financial impact ranges
- **3-Year TCO:** Total cost of ownership for comprehensive AEON program
- **Risk Reduction:** Prevented incident costs minus AEON investment
- **Additional Value:** Insurance savings, compliance efficiency, competitive advantage
- **Conservative Estimates:** Low-end assumes prevention of 1 major incident
