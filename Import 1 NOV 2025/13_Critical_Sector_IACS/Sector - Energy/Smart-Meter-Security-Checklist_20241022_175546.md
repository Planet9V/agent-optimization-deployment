# Smart Meter Security Checklist

Use this checklist to ensure you're taking all necessary steps to protect your smart meter and maintain the security of your electricity delivery.

## Physical Security

- [ ] Regularly inspect the smart meter for signs of tampering or damage
- [ ] Ensure the area around the meter is clear and accessible
- [ ] Report any suspicious activity or unauthorized attempts to access the meter
- [ ] Keep the meter's identification number in a safe place for reference

## Network Security (if applicable)

- [ ] Use a strong, unique password for your home Wi-Fi network
- [ ] Consider setting up a separate network for smart home devices, including your meter
- [ ] Keep your home router's firmware up-to-date
- [ ] Enable WPA3 encryption on your Wi-Fi network if supported

## Data Privacy

- [ ] Be cautious about sharing your energy usage information with third parties
- [ ] Regularly review your energy consumption data for any anomalies
- [ ] Understand what data is being collected and how it's being used (refer to Intellihub's privacy policy)

## Reporting and Communication

- [ ] Save Intellihub's contact information for reporting issues
- [ ] Report any unexpected changes in your energy readings promptly
- [ ] Inform Intellihub if you're planning any electrical work that might affect the meter

## Maintenance and Updates

- [ ] Allow scheduled maintenance and updates to ensure your meter has the latest security features
- [ ] Cooperate with Intellihub during any security audits or updates
- [ ] Participate in security awareness programs offered by Intellihub

## General Security Practices

- [ ] Be wary of unsolicited calls or visits from individuals claiming to be from the utility company
- [ ] Don't share your account details or meter information with unverified individuals
- [ ] Keep informed about the latest smart meter security best practices

By following this checklist, you play a crucial role in maintaining the security and integrity of your smart meter system. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact Intellihub's customer support team.

Remember: Your vigilance is key to protecting your smart meter and ensuring the safe, efficient delivery of your electricity.
