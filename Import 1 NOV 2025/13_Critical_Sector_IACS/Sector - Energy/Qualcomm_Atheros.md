# Qualcomm Atheros

Qualcomm Atheros is a subsidiary of Qualcomm that develops semiconductors for network communications, particularly wireless chipsets. The company was formed when Qualcomm acquired Atheros Communications in 2011.

## Key Information

- Parent Company: Qualcomm
- Headquarters: San Jose, California, United States
- Industry: Semiconductors, Wireless technology
- Products: Wi-Fi chipsets, Bluetooth chipsets, GPS chipsets

## Notable Products and Technologies

1. Wi-Fi chipsets (802.11 a/b/g/n/ac/ax)
2. Bluetooth chipsets
3. GPS chipsets
4. Power line communication (PLC) chipsets
5. Ethernet controllers

## Notable Developments

- In 2016, Qualcomm Atheros introduced the QCA4020 and QCA4024 system-on-chips (SoCs), integrating Wi-Fi, Bluetooth Low Energy 5, and 802.15.4-based technologies (including ZigBee and Thread).
- The company has been a significant contributor to the development of Wi-Fi 6 (802.11ax) technology.
