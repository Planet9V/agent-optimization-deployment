## Summary
Equipment maintenance procedures are standardized methodologies for ensuring the reliability, availability, and performance of critical manufacturing equipment. These procedures encompass preventive, predictive, corrective, and emergency maintenance approaches to minimize downtime, optimize equipment performance, and extend equipment lifespan. Effective maintenance procedures are essential for maintaining production efficiency, ensuring product quality, and preventing costly equipment failures in critical manufacturing environments.

## Key Information
- **Procedure Type**: Equipment Maintenance Operations
- **Maintenance Types**: Preventive, Predictive, Corrective, Emergency
- **Frequency**: Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, annual
- **Documentation**: Maintenance logs, work orders, checklists
- **Compliance**: ISO 55000, ISO 14224, OSHA standards
- **Technology**: CMMS, IoT sensors, predictive analytics

## Technical Details
### Maintenance Framework

#### 1. Maintenance Types
**Preventive Maintenance (PM)**
- **Scheduled Maintenance**: Time-based or usage-based maintenance activities
- **Inspection**: Regular equipment inspection and condition assessment
- **Cleaning**: Equipment cleaning, lubrication, and preventive adjustments
- **Replacement**: Component replacement before failure
- **Calibration**: Equipment calibration and verification
- **Documentation**: PM work orders, checklists, and completion records

**Predictive Maintenance (PdM)**
- **Condition Monitoring**: Real-time equipment condition monitoring
- **Vibration Analysis**: Vibration analysis for mechanical health assessment
- **Thermography**: Infrared thermography for thermal condition assessment
- **Oil Analysis**: Lubricant analysis for wear particle detection
- **Performance Monitoring**: Equipment performance metrics tracking
- **Predictive Analytics**: Data analysis for failure prediction and optimization

**Corrective Maintenance (CM)**
- **Breakdown Maintenance**: Unscheduled maintenance for equipment failures
- **Troubleshooting**: Systematic fault diagnosis and root cause analysis
- **Repair**: Component repair or replacement
- **Testing**: Post-repair testing and validation
- **Documentation**: CM work orders, failure analysis reports
- **Continuous Improvement**: Lessons learned and procedure updates

**Emergency Maintenance (EM)**
- **Emergency Response**: Immediate response to critical equipment failures
- **Rapid Assessment**: Quick assessment of failure impact and severity
- **Temporary Fixes**: Temporary measures to restore production
- **Permanent Repair**: Permanent repair solutions implementation
- **Root Cause Analysis**: Emergency root cause analysis
- **Preventive Actions**: Preventive actions to avoid recurrence

### Maintenance Procedures

- **Visual Inspection**: Visual inspection of equipment condition and safety features
- **Operational Check**: Operational check of equipment performance and functionality
- **Basic Cleaning**: Basic cleaning of equipment surfaces and critical areas
- **Lubrication**: Lubrication of moving parts and bearings
- **Parameter Check**: Check of critical parameters and settings
- **Documentation**: Documentation of daily maintenance activities and observations

**Weekly Maintenance Procedures**
- **Detailed Inspection**: Detailed inspection of equipment components and systems
- **Functional Testing**: Functional testing of equipment systems and controls
