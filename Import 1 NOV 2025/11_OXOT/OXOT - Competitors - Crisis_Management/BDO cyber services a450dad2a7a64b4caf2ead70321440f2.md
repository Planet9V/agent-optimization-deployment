Based on the search results, BDO does offer IT incident response capabilities and coordinates with organizations on cybersecurity and IT risk management. Here are some key points:

1. BDO provides Cyber Incident Response services, including:
- Rapid response forensic analysis, eradication, and remediation
- Continuous threat monitoring and analysis
- Advanced threat hunting
- Incident response operational readiness
- Incident response planning
1. They have a Cyber Incident Response Team that rapidly deploys and manages all aspects of a cyber investigation.
2. BDO offers incident response planning services to help organizations develop and test comprehensive incident response plans to minimize the impact of data breaches.
3. They provide 24/7 monitoring and situational awareness support as part of their crisis management services.
4. BDO helps organizations establish crisis response structures and formalize decision-making processes for incident response.
5. They offer managed IT security services that continuously validate cybersecurity measures are effectively defending against threats.
6. BDO coordinates with internal IT teams to complement their capabilities, for example by providing virtual DBA services to support SQL database management.
7. They take an integrated approach to risk management, incorporating industry best practices and coordinating between different specialized security agents.
8. BDO helps organizations develop incident response capabilities through training, exercises, and technology implementation.

So in summary, yes, BDO does coordinate closely with organizations' IT teams and provides comprehensive incident response capabilities as part of their cybersecurity and risk management services. They aim to integrate with and augment internal IT resources rather than replace them.
