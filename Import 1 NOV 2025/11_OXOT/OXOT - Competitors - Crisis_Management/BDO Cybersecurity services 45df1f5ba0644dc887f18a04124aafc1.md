Based on the search results, BDO offers the following cybersecurity architecture and transformation services:

Security Architecture & Transformation:

- Design and implement a cybersecurity architecture and framework tailored to business needs and the enterprise ecosystem.
- Encompasses access controls, entitlement, data protection, security monitoring, data privacy, and the selection and implementation of security tools.

Comprehensive Assessment:

- Evaluate digital assets and existing cybersecurity measures
- Provide clear, actionable directions for infrastructure costs and defenses

Third-Party Solution Optimization:

- Examine third-party solutions for opportunities to consolidate vendors
- Inventory licenses to reduce waste
1. Next-Generation Technology Readiness:
- Assess readiness for next-generation artificial intelligence applications

Penetration Testing:

- Conduct internal and external penetration testing using known attack vectors and zero-day exploits
- Identify security gaps and implement immediate corrections through software upgrades, network configuration changes, or security policy adjustments

Continuous Monitoring and Threat Response:

- Manage digital security from BDO's operations center
- Provide 24/7 monitoring for anomalies
- Respond quickly to alerts and take necessary actions
- Proactively inoculate systems against newly identified threats

Cloud Security:

- Help organizations leverage cloud technologies securely
- Provide services for cloud migration, governance, and management

Digital Transformation Support:

- Assist with broader digital strategy and transformation initiatives
- Ensure cybersecurity is integrated from the start of digital transformation projects

Compliance Alignment:

- Design and implement cybersecurity measures in alignment with relevant regulatory requirements and industry standards

These services are designed to help organizations build a robust, proactive, and adaptive cybersecurity architecture that can protect against evolving threats while supporting business objectives and digital transformation initiatives.
