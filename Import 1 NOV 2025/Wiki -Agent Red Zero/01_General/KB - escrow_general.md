


## Categories
### Personal or family-related software escrow arrangements, which can be used to protect and manage sensitive information or digital assets until specific triggering events occur.

This type of personal or [[family digital escrow]] arrangement can provide peace of mind for individuals concerned about the management and distribution of their digital assets and sensitive information. It ensures that important data, assets, and family legacies are protected and transferred according to the grantor's wishes, while also maintaining privacy and security until the specified triggering events occur.

### [[Multi-party Software [[Escrow]]
Multiparty software escrow is an arrangement that involves multiple parties in a software escrow agreement, typically designed to protect the interests of various stakeholders involved in software licensing or development.
