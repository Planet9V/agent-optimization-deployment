# NER11 Gold Standard - Full Entity Inventory

**Source**: `NER11_ENTITY_MASTER_LIST.md`
**Target Entities**: 566 (Design Goal)
**Actual Training Entities**: 60 (Validated 2025-11-26)
**Date**: 2025-11-26

> [!WARNING]
> **CRITICAL DISCREPANCY IDENTIFIED**: This document lists the **566** target entity types for the full "Gold Standard" design. However, a deep inspection of the actual training data (`train.spacy`) reveals that only **60** high-level labels are currently implemented. The granular types listed below (e.g., `RANSOMWARE`, `NATION_STATE`) are currently subsumed under parent labels (e.g., `MALWARE`, `THREAT_ACTOR`) or missing.

This document provides a complete, flat inventory of all entity types **intended** for the NER11 Gold Standard model, organized by their semantic tiers.
