DATABASE SCHEMA
════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Neo4j Nodes:
- SBOM (with super_label: Asset, tier: TIER2)
- Component (with super_label: Asset, tier: TIER3)

Neo4j Relationships:
- (:SBOM)-[:CONTAINS]->(:Component)
- (:Component)-[:DEPENDS_ON]->(:Component)
- (:Component)-[:HAS_VULNERABILITY]->(:CVE)

Qdrant Collection:
- Name: sbom_components
- Vector Size: 768 (BERT-compatible)
- Distance: COSINE
- Payload: component metadata + customer_id
