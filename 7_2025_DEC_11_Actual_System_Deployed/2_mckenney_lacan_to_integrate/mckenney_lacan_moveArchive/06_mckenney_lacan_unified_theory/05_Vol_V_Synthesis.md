# Chapter 15: Critical Slowing Down (Early Warning Systems)

How do we predict the Phase Transition (Revolution/Crash)?

## 15.1 The Recovery Rate ($\lambda$)

When a stable system is perturbed, it returns to equilibrium at rate $\lambda$.

* **Pre-Critical State**: $\lambda \to 0$. The system becomes "sluggish." It "remembers" the shock for a long time.

## 15.2 The Observable Metrics

We generally cannot measure $\lambda$ directly, but we can measure:

1. **Variance Spike**: $\text{Var}(Y_t) \to \infty$. (Wild swings in public sentiment).
2. **Lag-1 Autocorrelation**: $AC(1) \to 1$. (Today's panic is highly correlated with yesterday's panic).
