## 3. CRISPR-Cas9 (The Editor)

The "Chef" acts as the **Cas9 Protein**.
*   **Guide RNA (gRNA)**: The Policy/Rule.
*   **Target**: A specific sequence of "Trauma" (e.g., `AAAA` - Aggression).
*   **Action**: Double-Strand Break (DSB) and Homology-Directed Repair (HDR).
*   **Result**: The Chef literally "edits" the personality of the system by cutting out the toxic traits and inserting healthy ones.
