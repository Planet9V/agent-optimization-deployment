## The 16 Confirmed Critical Infrastructure Sectors

As designated by PPD-21 (2013) and reaffirmed by NSM-22 (2024):

1. Chemical
2. Commercial Facilities
3. Communications
4. Critical Manufacturing
5. Dams
6. Defense Industrial Base
7. Emergency Services
8. Energy
9. Financial Services
10. Food and Agriculture
11. Government Facilities
12. Healthcare and Public Health
13. Information Technology
14. Nuclear Reactors, Materials, and Waste
15. Transportation Systems
16. Water and Wastewater Systems

**Note:** The 2024 National Security Memorandum explicitly stated "does not add any new sectors" despite consideration of Space and Bioeconomy sectors.

## Subsector Count Research

### ⚠️ CRITICAL FINDING: NO VERIFICATION OF ~335 SUBSECTORS

After extensive search across multiple authoritative sources, **I could NOT find any official documentation supporting a count of "approximately 335 subsectors."**

### What I DID Find:

#### Infrastructure Data Taxonomy (IDT) Structure
- **5-level hierarchy:** Sector → Sub-sector → Segment → Sub-segment → Asset type
- **Source:** CISA Infrastructure Data Taxonomy v4 (2011)
- **URL:** https://www.cisa.gov/resources-tools/resources/infrastructure-data-taxonomy
- **PDF:** https://www.cisa.gov/sites/default/files/cisa/IDT/Taxonomy_V4_Final_22Jun2011.pdf (unable to parse)

#### Confirmed Subsector Examples by Sector

| Sector | Subsector Count | Subsector Names | Source |
|--------|-----------------|-----------------|---------|
| **Transportation Systems** | 7 | Aviation, Highway/Motor Carrier, Maritime, Mass Transit/Passenger Rail, Pipeline, Freight Rail, Postal/Shipping | CISA Official |
| **Commercial Facilities** | 8 | Entertainment/Media, Gaming, Lodging, Outdoor Events, Public Assembly, Real Estate, Retail, Sports Leagues | CISA Official |
| **Critical Manufacturing** | 4 | Primary Metals, Machinery, Electrical Equipment/Appliance/Components, Transportation Equipment | CISA Official |
| **Healthcare and Public Health** | 8 | 6 private subsectors + 2 government subsectors (not individually named) | SSP 2016 |
| **Energy** | 3 | Electricity, Oil, Natural Gas | Energy SSP 2015 |
| **Government Facilities** | 1+ | Education Facilities Subsector (as annex) | SSP |

**Total from confirmed sources: ~31-35 subsectors** (across only 6 of 16 sectors)

### Where the "335" Number MAY Have Come From

1. **NAICS Code Confusion:** NAICS code 335 = "Electrical Equipment, Appliance, and Component Manufacturing" (unrelated to critical infrastructure taxonomy)

2. **Possible Segment/Sub-segment Count:** The IDT includes 5 levels (Sector → Sub-sector → Segment → Sub-segment → Asset type). The "335" might refer to total **segments** or **sub-segments** across all sectors, NOT subsectors.

3. **Undocumented Internal Classification:** May exist in restricted/internal DHS documents not publicly available.

4. **Confusion with Asset Counts:** Could be confused with counts of specific critical assets rather than subsector classifications.
