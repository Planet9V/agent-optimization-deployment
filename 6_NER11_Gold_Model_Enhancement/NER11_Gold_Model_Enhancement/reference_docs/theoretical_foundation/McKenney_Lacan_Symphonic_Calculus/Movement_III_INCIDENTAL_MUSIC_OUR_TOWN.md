## 1. The Motif (The Mundane)

Barber's music for *Our Town* is intentionally simple. No grand fanfares. No complex counterpoint. Just a simple melody on an oboe, supported by warm strings.
*   **The Sound**: *Morning in Grover's Corners.*
*   **The Meaning**: Life is happening. Ordinary, boring, beautiful life.
