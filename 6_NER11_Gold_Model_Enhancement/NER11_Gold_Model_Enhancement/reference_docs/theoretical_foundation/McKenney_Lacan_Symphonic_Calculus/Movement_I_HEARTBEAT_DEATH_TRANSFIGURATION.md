## 1. The Motif (Largo)

Strauss begins *Tod und Verklärung* with a syncopated, irregular rhythm in the timpani and strings.
*   **The Sound**: *Lub-dub... Lub-dub... ... ... Lub-dub.*
*   **The Meaning**: The artist is dying. The heart is failing.

## 2. The Organic Calculus

We define the **System Heartbeat ($H(t)$)**.
$$ H(t) = \sum_i \delta(t - t_i) $$
*   **Regularity**: In a healthy system, $t_{i+1} - t_i \approx \text{Constant}$.
*   **Arrhythmia**: As the system fails (Memory Leak, CPU Starvation), the interval becomes chaotic.

### 2.1 The Arrhythmia Metric ($\alpha$)
$$ \alpha = \text{Var}(t_{i+1} - t_i) $$
*   **$\alpha \approx 0$**: Healthy.
*   **$\alpha > 0$**: Sick (Jitter).
*   **$\alpha \to \infty$**: Cardiac Arrest (Timeout).
