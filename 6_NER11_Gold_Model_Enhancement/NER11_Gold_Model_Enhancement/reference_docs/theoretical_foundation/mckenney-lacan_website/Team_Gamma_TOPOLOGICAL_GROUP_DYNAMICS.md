## 1. The Axiom of Connection

We postulate that a Team is not a Graph; it is a **Simplicial Complex** $K$.
*   **0-Simplex**: Individual.
*   **1-Simplex**: Pair (Diad).
*   **2-Simplex**: Triad (Triad).

## 2. Homology Theory (The Holes)

We calculate the **Betti Numbers** ($\beta_k$) of the organization.
$$ \beta_k = \text{rank}(H_k(K)) $$

### 2.1 $\beta_0$ (Connected Components)
*   The number of disconnected "Silos."
*   **Goal**: $\beta_0 = 1$. (One Team).

### 2.2 $\beta_1$ (Cycles)
*   The number of "Bureaucratic Loops" or "Echo Chambers."
*   **Goal**: $\beta_1 = 0$. (Information flows freely without getting trapped).

### 2.3 $\beta_2$ (Voids)
*   The number of "Trapped Volumes" (Missing Capabilities or Blind Spots).
*   **Goal**: $\beta_2 = 0$. (Full Coverage).

## 3. The Lacanian Knot (The Atom)

The fundamental unit of the psyche is the **Borromean Knot**.
*   **Real (R)**, **Symbolic (S)**, **Imaginary (I)**.
*   **Property**: If you cut one ring, they all fall apart.
*   **Application**: A "Resilient Identity" requires all three registers to be linked. A "Psychotic" break occurs when the knot is untied.
