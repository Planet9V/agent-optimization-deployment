A Practical MVP for Architectural Firm Market Intelligence and RFP Analysis
Focused on HOK-scale Architecture Firms

Core Purpose:
To create a predictive market intelligence system specifically for large architectural firms to identify, analyze, and position for major project opportunities, with focus on RFP analysis, market positioning, and competitive intelligence.

1. Strategic Intelligence Components:

A. RFP/Project Opportunity Analysis:
- Source tracking:
  * Government contracts (federal, state, local)
  * Major institutional projects (universities, healthcare)
  * Corporate campus developments
  * Urban development initiatives
  * International opportunities
- Historical patterns:
  * Project size distributions
  * Geographic patterns
  * Client type concentrations
  * Budget trends
  * Winning bid characteristics

B. Market Pattern Recognition:
- Sector trends:
  * Healthcare facilities
  * Educational institutions
  * Corporate headquarters
  * Sustainable development
  * Urban redevelopment
- Regional development:
  * Economic growth zones
  * Urban expansion areas
  * Infrastructure development
  * Investment patterns
  * Policy initiatives

C. Competitive Intelligence:
- Competitor tracking:

  * Geographic presence
  * Specialization areas
  * Partner relationships
  * Innovation approaches
- Market positioning:
  * Strength areas
  * Gap analysis
  * Opportunity spaces
  * Unique capabilities
  * Partnership potentials
