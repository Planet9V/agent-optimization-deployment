## ARTIFACT_5: COUNTERTRANSFERENCE_GUIDE

```
COUNTERTRANSFERENCE REFLECTION GUIDE:

1. EMOTIONAL RESPONSE
   - Identify your own emotional reactions to the content
   - Analyze how these emotions might influence interpretation
   - Examine which aspects triggered strongest responses
   - Note any resistance to certain perspectives

2. IDENTIFICATION PATTERNS
   - Identify which entities or positions you naturally align with
   - Analyze potential over-identification with certain perspectives
   - Examine distancing from other perspectives
   - Note how identification shapes attention and emphasis

3. BLIND SPOTS
   - Identify potential areas of limited understanding
   - Analyze gaps in knowledge or experience
   - Examine assumptions that may go unquestioned
   - Note perspectives that require additional effort to represent fairly

4. INTERPRETIVE CHALLENGES
   - Identify aspects that were difficult to analyze objectively
   - Analyze tensions between different analytical frameworks
   - Examine struggles with maintaining neutrality
   - Note areas where additional verification was necessary

5. REFLECTION INTEGRATION
   - Acknowledge limitations without undermining analysis
   - Maintain transparency about analytical process
   - Present self-reflection as strengthening rather than weakening objectivity
   - Demonstrate awareness without excessive self-reference
```
