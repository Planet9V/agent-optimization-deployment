## ARTIFACT_4: OBJECTIVITY_CHECKLIST

```
OBJECTIVITY VERIFICATION:

1. LANGUAGE
□ Uses descriptive rather than prescriptive language
□ Avoids loaded terms and emotional language
□ Maintains consistent tone across all perspectives
□ Presents analysis as observation rather than judgment

1. BALANCE
□ Presents at least three distinct perspectives
□ Allocates approximately equal space to each perspective
□ Includes both rationales and concerns for each perspective
□ Uses parallel structure when presenting different viewpoints

1. FACTUAL FOCUS
□ Emphasizes verifiable facts and documented positions
□ Includes relevant statistics and data points when available
□ Cites sources for claims and positions
□ Distinguishes between facts and interpretations

1. NEUTRALITY
□ Avoids advocating for any policy position
□ Presents media patterns as observations without criticism
□ Describes rather than evaluates different perspectives
□ Maintains distance from all ideological positions

1. CONNECTION OBJECTIVITY
□ Presents connections as factual relationships
□ Describes institutional and historical connections without implied causality
□ Presents multiple possible interpretations of observed connections
□ Focuses on formal rather than ideological relationships

1. BIAS AWARENESS
□ Identifies specific framing devices without judgment
□ Notes perspectives that are centered or marginalized as observation
□ Recognizes emotional appeals and their targets without evaluation
□ Acknowledges ideological markers without endorsement or criticism

1. PSYCHOANALYTIC NEUTRALITY
□ Applies psychoanalytic concepts as analytical tools rather than judgments
□ Presents multiple interpretations of psychological patterns
□ Avoids pathologizing any perspective or position
□ Maintains analytical distance while applying theoretical frameworks
