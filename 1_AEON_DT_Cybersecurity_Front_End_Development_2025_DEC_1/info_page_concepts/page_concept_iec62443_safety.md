# Page Concept: The Shield Generator
**"Defense in Depth. Visualized."**

## 1. Core Concept
A visualization of the IEC 62443 Safety Zones as a series of concentric, glowing force fields protecting the core assets. It turns abstract compliance data into a tangible "Shield Status" display.

## 2. Visual Design (The "Star Wars" Diagnostics)
*   **Style**: Tactical, military-grade interface. Green/Amber/Red status lights. Rotating shield emitters.
*   **Metaphor**: "The Fortress".

## 3. The Layers (Zones)

### The Perimeter (Enterprise Zone - Level 4)
*   **Visual**: The outermost shield. Large, transparent, fluctuating.
*   **Status**: "Shield Integrity: 98%". "Threats Deflected: 14,200".

### The Inner Wall (Site Ops - Level 3)
*   **Visual**: A denser, hexagonal grid shield.
*   **Interaction**: Click to see "Conduits" (tunnels) passing through.

### The Core (Control Zone - Level 1 & 0)
*   **Visual**: The "Reactor Core". A blindingly bright, heavily armored vault.
*   **Highlight**: "Air Gap Status: Verified".

## 4. The Compliance Scanner
*   **Visual**: A scanning beam sweeping across the shields.
*   **Data**:
    *   `Scanning Zone 2...`
    *   `Foundational Requirement 5 (Restricted Data Flow): FAILED.`
    *   `Gap: Unencrypted Modbus Traffic detected.`
    *   `Remediation Cost: $15,000.`

## 5. Technical Implementation
*   **Data Source**: `Enhancement_07_IEC62443_Safety/README.md`.
*   **Visuals**: WebGL shaders for the force field effects.

## 6. Innovation Factor
*   **"Gamification"**: Makes compliance feel like managing a starship's defenses.
*   **"Clarity"**: Instantly communicates "Defense in Depth" (multiple layers).
*   **"Urgency"**: A red "Shield Failure" alert is visceral and demands action.
