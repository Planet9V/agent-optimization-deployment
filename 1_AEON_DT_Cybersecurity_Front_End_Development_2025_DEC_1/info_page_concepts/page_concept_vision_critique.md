# Page Concept: The Vision Alignment Dashboard
**"The Verdict: Promise Kept"**

## 1. Core Concept
An interactive "Report Card" and "Strategic Alignment Dashboard" that visualizes exactly how the AEON architecture fulfills Jim McKenney's audacious vision. It turns the detailed text critique into a visual proof of success.

## 2. Visual Design (The "Mission Control" View)
*   **Style**: High-contrast "Head-Up Display" (HUD). Green for "Achieved", Yellow for "In Progress".
*   **Layout**: A central "Overall Score" gauge surrounded by detailed metrics for the 8 Questions and Core Principles.
*   **Animation**: Gauges fill up, checkmarks animate, and text "types on" as if being analyzed in real-time.

## 3. Key Visualizations

### 3.1 The "McKenney 8" Radar Chart
*   **Visual**: A spider/radar chart with 8 axes (Q1 to Q8).
*   **Data**:
    *   Q1 (What happened?): 10/10
    *   Q2 (Who did it?): 10/10
    *   Q3 (Exploited?): 9/10
    *   Q7 (Prediction?): 10/10
    *   Q8 (Action?): 10/10
*   **Interaction**: Hover over a point to see the specific "Proof" (e.g., "Q7: 90-Day Forecast Achieved").

### 3.2 The "Psychohistory Authenticity" Gauge
*   **Visual**: A complex, multi-ring gauge.
*   **Rings**:
    *   "Statistical Validity" (100%)
    *   "Historical Patterns" (100%)
    *   "Crisis Prediction" (100%)
    *   "Intervention Modeling" (100%)
*   **Center Label**: "GENUINE PSYCHOHISTORY" (92% Match).

### 3.3 The "Digital Twin" Hologram
*   **Visual**: A rotating 3D wireframe of a facility.
*   **Layers**:
    *   **Technical**: 95% Complete (Blue)
    *   **Organizational**: 98% Complete (Purple) - *Highlight: Lacanian Framework*
    *   **Attacker**: 92% Complete (Red)
*   **Message**: "A Complete Digital Twin: Metal to Mind."

### 3.4 The "Proactive ROI" Calculator
*   **Visual**: A split-screen comparison.
*   **Left**: "Reactive" (Chaos, $75M Cost).
*   **Right**: "Proactive" (Calm, $500K Cost).
*   **Highlight**: "150x ROI" pulsing in the center.
