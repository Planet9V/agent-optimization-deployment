## 2. Core Concept: "The Human Firewall"
This page argues that the biggest vulnerability isn't code; it's people. But people are also the strongest defense. It visualizes the "Human Layer" of the stack.

## 3. The "Wow" Visualization
**The Collective Mind Map**
*   **Visual**: A glowing, interconnected network of "Nodes" (People).
*   **Interaction**:
    *   **The Team**: Grouping nodes reveals their "Chemical Formula" (Team Fit).
    *   **The Dissonance**: A "Stress Test" button applies pressure to the network. Nodes with high "Cognitive Dissonance" vibrate and turn red.
    *   **The Resolution**: Applying a "Training Intervention" stabilizes the network.

## 5. Integrated Features
*   **The Team Alchemist**: (From Alchemy of Teams) A drag-and-drop tool. "What happens if we move Agent Smith to the Blue Team?" -> *Simulation shows +15% Resilience.*
*   **The Dissonance Radar**: (From Cognitive Dissonance) A heatmap of the organization showing pockets of "Cultural Stress."
*   **The Lacan Knot**: (From Lacan Calculus) A simplified 3D visualization showing the balance between Real, Symbolic, and Imaginary in the corporate culture.

## 6. Technical Implementation
*   **Stack**: D3.js (Network Graph) + WebGL (Fluid Dynamics for the "Alchemy" mixing effects).
*   **Privacy**: "Anonymized Mode" toggle is prominent to reassure HR stakeholders.
