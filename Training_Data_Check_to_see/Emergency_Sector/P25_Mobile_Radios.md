- **High-Gain Mobile Antenna**: 5/8 wave, 3.0-5.0 dBd gain
- **GPS Antenna**: Active GPS antenna with 28 dB gain, SMA connector
- **NMO Mount**: Standard 3/4" hole mount for vehicle installation
