## 14. Citation Format Guide

### APA Format Requirements:

**Books:**
```
Author(s). (Year). Title of book. Publisher.
```

**Journal Articles:**
```
Author(s). (Year). Title of article. Journal Name, Volume(Issue), pages.
https://doi.org/[DOI]
```

**Websites:**
```
Organization. (Year). Title of webpage. Retrieved from https://www.example.com
```

**Government Documents:**
```
Department/Agency. (Year). Document title (Publication number).
Publisher. https://doi.org/[DOI]
```
