### Battery Technology Comparison
- **IMPRES (Motorola)**: Intelligent battery management, health tracking, runtime prediction
- **Smart Battery (L3Harris)**: Battery health monitoring, charge cycle tracking
- **Li-Ion Technology**: Higher energy density than NiMH, no memory effect
- **Typical Capacity Range**: 1850 mAh to 3500 mAh
- **Typical Runtime**: 10-18 hours (5-5-90 duty cycle)
- **Charge Time**: 2-4 hours for full charge
- **Battery Life**: 500-800 charge cycles typical

### Environmental Ratings Explained
- **IP67**: Dust-tight, submersible 1 meter for 30 minutes
- **IP68**: Dust-tight, submersible 2+ meters for extended periods
- **MIL-STD-810**: Military standard for environmental testing
- **Operating Temperature**: Typical -30°C to +60°C
- **Humidity**: Up to 95% non-condensing
- **Shock**: Drop test from 1-2 meters onto concrete
- **Vibration**: Simulated vehicle vibration testing
